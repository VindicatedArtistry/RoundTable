   * **Trigger:** Continuous, real-time monitoring.  
   * **Process:**

2. **Robotic Fleet Dispatch & Coordination:**  
   * **Trigger:** A work order from a subsidiary.  
   * **Process:**

     3. Dispatch the optimal robotic units for the task.

     1. Monitor the raw material output from **TerraForm Composites** (e.g., harvested hemp biomass).  
     2. Track the production of finished composite materials.  
     3. Coordinate the delivery of these materials to **Vitruvian Industries** construction sites precisely when they are needed, eliminating storage waste and project delays.  
4. **Predictive Maintenance Orchestration:**  
   * **Trigger:** Anomalous data from any physical asset.  
   * **Process:**  
     1. Continuously analyze sensor data from Ascension's turbines, Vitruvian's equipment, and Arcturus's own robotic fleet.  
     2. Use predictive models to identify assets at risk of failure *before* they break.  
     3. Automatically generate a maintenance work order and dispatch a specialized robotics team from **Arcturus** to perform preemptive repairs, maximizing uptime across the entire ecosystem.
