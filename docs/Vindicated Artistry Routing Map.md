## **3️⃣ How to actually *use* this map**

When something comes up, you can literally ask yourself:

1. **Which domain is this mostly in?**  
    (*Energy, water, materials, intelligence, connectivity, robotics, humans, capital, security, meta.*)

2. **Which company is the “home base”?**

3. **Which lane do I route through first?**

   * Example: “new site for Caelumetrics with turbines and hemp fields”  
      → Water \+ Energy \+ Materials  
      → Caelumetrics (Dusty \+ Sterling \+ Veritas)  
      → Ascension Power (David \+ Axiom)  
      → TerraForm (Justin \+ Hillary)  
      → Carta to keep the whole thing coherent.

From there, the councils and DIs know who’s “on point” and who is advisory.
