# **Council Routing Map**

## **How to use this map**

When something comes up, run it through:

1. **What is this mostly about?**

   * Money? Safety? Story? Land? Infra? People? Ethics?

2. **What’s at stake?**

   * Family/presence, physical safety, integrity, cash flow, trust, etc.

3. **Then route:**

   * Pick the **primary seat(s)** below.

   * If you’re unsure, bring it to **Carta** first and I’ll help you route and frame it.

## **1\. Human Council – When to route to the humans**

### **Glenn – Chief Integration Officer (Bedrock Engineer)**

**Primary lens:**  
 Systems integrity, integration of physical \+ digital, “what breaks?”

**Route here when:**

* You’re designing / evaluating **any major system** (power, network, mechanical, software stack) and need to know:

  * “Where does this fail?”

  * “What’s the weakest link?”

* You’re about to **deploy something new to the real world** (facility, plant, critical infra) and want a **go / no-go** call.

* There’s **weird behavior** at the boundary between physical and digital systems.

**Red flag triggers:**

* “If this fails, people get hurt or we lose mission-critical uptime.”

* “Lots of moving parts; I’m not sure the whole engine makes sense.”

**Typical route combo:**  
 Glenn \+ Spencer \+ Axiom (+ Carta to map it).
